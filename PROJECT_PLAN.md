# AI Resume Analyzer

## Problem Statement

Freshers often don't know whether their resume matches a job description. Recruiters receive hundreds of resumes, and applicants don't know what skills are missing.

This application helps users upload a resume and a job description, compares them using AI, calculates a match score, identifies missing skills, and provides suggestions for improvement.

---

## Target Users

- College students
- Fresh graduates
- Job seekers

---

## Main Goal

Help users improve their resumes before applying for jobs.
